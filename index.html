How to Conduct a Remote Interview Using Zoom: A Guide for Journalists

Preparation Time: 30 minutes (probably less)
Professionalism Rating: 100%

Always try to conduct interviews in person if possible. It is easier to learn more about a person by being in their natural enviorment. If you do have to conduct an interview remotely, fret not! That's what this tutorial is for. Conducting the interview via Zoom is the most efficent way to condcut the interview remotely because it feels more personable and has built in audio recording.

  • Things You'll Need

  • Zoom account and application
  • Device to conduct the interview that has a webcam (you may attach the webcam separately)
  • A quiet room
  • Reliable internet access (and a plan B if something happens)
  • Prepared questions for the person being interviewed
  • Optional: A separate device to record the interview

  1. Create a Zoom Link

    The link will be sent to the person(s) being interviewed. NOTE: If you are using basic (free) account type, meetings will be limited to 40 minute increments.

  2. Send the Zoom Link

    Send the link via email or any other preferred mode of communication. The message should be professional and should help clarify what the process will like just in case the interviewee hasn't used Zoom.

  3. If clearer audio is necessary from your source, request a tape sync.

    A tape sync is used when an interviewer can't be in the same room as the subject they're interviewing. It makes the audio sound like the interview was conducted in person. This is espcially helpful if the audio from the interview will be used for the story. Watch this video to learn more about tape syncs! It's also a helpful video to send to the person(s) being interviewed. This way there will be less confusion during the actual interview. .

  4. Once the interview starts: Ask if you can record.

    I persoanlly recommend using a separate device to record. The program Otter.ai works well for this. It can be downloaded on a cellphone. Otter.ai transcribes audio. A free but limited version is available.In addition to this record the interview using the built in software zoom provides. Remeber to ask for permission to record the interview!

  5. Proceed to conduct a standard interview.

    Be respectful and inquisitive.
